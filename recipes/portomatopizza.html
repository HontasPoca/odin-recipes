PortoMato Pizza- Your Beautiful Red and Brown Delightfull Pizza

Description

This Pizza, is my new favorite one, on top of Pizza being the most favorite food, for me. The style, the sharp colors of fresh vegetables, and the smell.... oooh the smell.! Grilled cheese on the surface, between and under the colors of this beautiful painting, adds a top notch one smelly and tasty Pizza. Brown. Red. white pastry. What more can you ask for in the perfect meal. Maybe some greens on the side.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pizza package of three!
  • 2 Fresh red Tomatoes!
  • Brown Fresh Portobella Mushrooms
  • Oven ready, heated up for 225 celsius degree

Steps

  1. First, make a good amount of tomato slices. Medium fine layers, in stick a like shape.
  2. Prepare Medium sliced Portobello Mushroom units.
  3. Take out the single pizza unit out of the Pizza package.
  4. Simply scatter The sliced Porto-Mato toppings on top and put it in the preheated oven.
  5. Wait for the enormous smell that you cant stand and ENJOY in 8-slices format.
  6. Average cooking time: 20:00 minutes.
